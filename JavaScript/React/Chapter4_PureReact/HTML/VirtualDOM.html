Baked Salmon

  • 1 lb Salmon
  • 1 cup Pine Nuts
  • 2 cupts Butter Lettuce
  • 1 Yellow Squash
  • 1/2 cup Olive Oil
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic

Cooking Instructions

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees

Spread the olive oil around a glass baking dish

Add the salmon, garlic, and pine nuts to the dish

Bake for 15 minutes

Add the yellow squash and put back in the oven for 30 mins Add Letuce and servce
